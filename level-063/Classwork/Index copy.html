Imeruli Khachapuri

Here you will learn how to make a Khachapuri.

    Preparation Time

  • Total: 1 Hour or more
  • Cooking: 20 Minutes
  • Preparation: Rest of the time
    •

Ingredients

    ღუმელი
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Matsoni
  • Butter
  • Imeruli Cheese
    •

Instructions

  • In a bowl add Flour, Salt, Yeast;
  • Then add Matsoni or Milk;
  • After that, knead this mixture;
  • And wait until the dough is finished;
  • Grind some cheese;
  • Flatten the dough;
  • Put the cheese onto the dough;
  • Wrap the cheese with the dough;
  • Flatten the dough;
  • Put this in the oven for 20 minutes at 220℃;
  • Finally, take it out and spread your butter onto the Khachapuri.
